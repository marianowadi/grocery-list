Privacy Policy for Grocery List - Shop Easy

Last Updated: December 12, 2025

Thank you for using Grocery List - Shop Easy. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how the app handles information.

1. No Data Collection

My Shopping List does not collect, store, or share personal data. We do not gather information about you, your device, or how you use the app.

2. Data Stored on Your Device Only

All data you create within Grocery List - Shop Easy, such as shopping lists, notes, or preferences — is stored locally on your device. No information is transmitted to external servers or cloud services for storage.

3. In‑Device Voice Recognition & OCR

The app may provide features like voice recognition (speech‑to‑text) and text recognition from pictures (OCR). These features are processed entirely on your device, using on‑device iOS frameworks. No audio recordings, images, recognized text, or derived data are sent to external servers during these operations.

4. Minimal External Communication (Version Check Only)

My Shopping List does not use analytics, advertising networks, cloud databases, or third‑party tracking services.

The app makes a single external request to check whether a newer version is available. This request is sent to our own server and is limited to version information only. No personal information or app content is transmitted as part of this check. No third‑party services are involved in this process.

5. No Social Login and No Tracking

My Shopping List does not offer or require social media logins, account creation, or any tracking technologies such as cookies, device fingerprinting, or unique advertising identifiers. The app functions fully offline except for the version check described above.

6. Children’s Privacy

Because the app does not collect personal data, My Shopping List does not knowingly collect information from children. The app is suitable for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post a revised version here and update the Last Updated date.
